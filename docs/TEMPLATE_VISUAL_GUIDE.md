## Design Philosophy Summary

### Corporate Elite
**Philosophy**: "Maximum credibility through conservative design"
- Traditional layout trusted by Fortune 500
- Serif headings add gravitas
- Navy blue conveys trust and stability
- Maximum white space = maximum ATS score

### Strategic Leader
**Philosophy**: "Leadership through visual hierarchy"
- Two-column shows organizational thinking
- Sidebar = quick scanning of credentials
- Teal represents innovation within professionalism
- Left borders guide the eye through achievements

### Impact Pro
**Philosophy**: "Bold results demand bold presentation"
- Green = growth and success
- Header blocks = clear section boundaries
- Emojis = modern, human touch
- Metrics first = results-oriented
