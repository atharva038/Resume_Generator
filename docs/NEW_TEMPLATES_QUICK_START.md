## üí° Pro Tips

### Maximize ATS Score:
1. Use **Corporate Elite** for maximum compatibility
2. Fill out all relevant sections completely
3. Use standard job titles and keywords
4. Include measurable achievements
5. Avoid images or complex graphics

### Stand Out:
1. Use **Impact Pro** for sales/marketing roles
2. Quantify all achievements with metrics
3. Use action verbs at the start of bullets
4. Customize for each application
5. Keep it to 1-2 pages maximum

### Leadership Roles:
1. Use **Strategic Leader** for executive positions
2. Focus on strategic impact and outcomes
3. Highlight team leadership and mentorship
4. Include cross-functional achievements
5. Emphasize business results
